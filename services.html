Dealership Assessment

The purpose of the assessment is to inspect and identify the areas of greatest concern and opportunity to maximize your return on investment. We respect your time and ours expediting profitability with a long term focus on continuous improvements A typical review may focus on the following:

Dealership Executive Management

Senior management meeting to discuss dealership customer satisfaction and retention. Reviewing and communication of your current business plan. Review of current objectives or concerns. Understanding your benchmarks in management process and financial management.

Vehicle Sales

Showroom Processes, Marketing & Advertising, Prospecting, Phone & Internet Leads, Sales Management, Unsold Prospects, F&I, Delivery, profit and sales analysis, review CSI index scores

Service

Service sales process, marketing, customer handling and retention initiatives, write-up & Inspection, shop efficiency, service gross profit and expense analysis, review CSI index scores

Parts

Shop distribution, order times, gross profit and expense analysis, inventory Metrics, special order parts, accessory marketing wholesale and sublet, review CSI index scores

We reconvene at assessment conclusion. We review our findings to determine remediation efforts towards greater profitability and customer experience.
