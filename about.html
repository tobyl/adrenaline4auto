Mike Nelson

Founder of Adrenaline Automotive Consultants

Mike Nelson has 25+ years of experience in the auto dealership industry, including sales, sales management, fixed operations, general management and ownership of a Ford Lincoln dealership.

The automotive business is second nature to Mike who was mentored by his father Harold an industry leader and respected fixed operations director. Mike was able to learn and develop his fixed operations understanding starting as detailer with progressive positions on the way to fixed operations manager of the dealership’s evening operations while attending college.

His studies included general management, leadership and coaching. Mike has attended many industry specific courses dealing with all aspects of dealer operations.

After selling his store Mike sought to assist others as a consultant for several major national firms.

Mike believed there was a better way to provide dealer process solutions customized to the current requirements of dealers and accordingly founded Adrenaline Automotive Consulting.

Observations

Mike was astonished that many businesses lack management training, very seldom provide leadership training, and offer negligible coaching training. We are always too busy is an accepted argument. The training attempted was commonly complimentary from a vendor to sell their merchandise exclusively. The major resource of manufacture driven initiatives tended to be a one dimensional solution. Mike developed his plan to assist others prosper by contributing experience and best practices of automotive dealers. His goal is to look at the entire dealership through the eyes of the consumer in order to provide an exceptional customer experience that will capitalize on every revenue stream realistic for your store with regard to its situation and way of life.

Bottom Line

Long term profitability and customer experience are a click away.
